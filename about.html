About Me

Claire Lueking is passionate about the intersection of technology, user experience, and design, thriving on problem-solving to create seamless digital experiences. With a background in Integrative Physiology from the University of Colorado Boulder, she is now pivoting into tech by pursuing a degree in Applied Computer Science. Her love for intuitive design emerged through coding in Python and HTML, as well as using Figma and Adobe Creative Cloud for visually engaging designs. An outdoor enthusiast and athlete, she embraces challenges both physically and intellectually. Claire is actively seeking opportunities in UX/UI design, computer science, and web development to blend her technical skills with user-centered innovation.
